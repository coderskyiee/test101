Arctic sea ice extent hits record low for winter maximum

CNN

1 hour ago

Environment

According to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration, Ted Scambos, NSIDC, lead scientist, puts the potential record low maximum sea ice extent this year down to low ice extent in the Pacific and a late drop in ice extent in the Barents Sea.

"Over a span of the satellite record. Arctic sea ice has been declined significantly, while sea ice in the arctic has increased very slightly."



-NOAA
